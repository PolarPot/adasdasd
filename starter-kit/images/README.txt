📁 IMAGES FOLDER — GDSC-UTP Workshop
======================================

Put your profile photo in THIS folder!

Steps:
  1. Copy your photo into this folder (e.g. profile.jpg)
  2. Open index.html
  3. Find the <img> tag in the #about section
  4. Change the src to:   src="images/profile.jpg"
  5. Change the alt to:   alt="Profile photo of [Your Name]"
  6. Save and refresh your browser — done!

Tips:
  - Any image format works: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
  - Keep the filename simple — no spaces! (use profile.jpg not my photo.jpg)
  - Recommended size: around 400x400 pixels or smaller
  - Crop it to a square for the best circle effect

Example:
  <img src="images/profile.jpg" alt="Profile photo of Ahmad" />
